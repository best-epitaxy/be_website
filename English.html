FACE RECOGNITION

BIG DATA

best Epitaxy Manufacturing Co. Ltd. was founded in November, 2017 with a 2,233 square-meter factory located in Zhunan Science Park. Currently it has few Aixtron MOCVD machines and more than 40 employees to produce 4" and 6" epitaxial wafers.

best Epitaxy Manufacturing Co. Ltd. acquired BE Epitaxy Semiconductor Technology Co. Ltd., which was led by a high-resolution epitaxy and chip design team and with the investment of capital, high-end technology, and the purchase of new machines. It’s expected that the production capacity will be expanded by 3 times within 2 years, and the paid-in capital will be doubled from NT$250 million to NT$500 million. The total number of employees will also be increased.

best Epitaxy Manufacturing Co. Ltd. will adhere to business philosophies of the most outstanding quality, technology, service, and value. With the dedication of new capital, innovative technology, and outstanding management, in addition to the original LED and P-Senor VCSEL epitaxial wafer, best Epitaxy Manufacturing Co. Ltd. will provide 14G & 25G GaAs VCSEL for optical communication and the development of 1.3μm, 1.5μm EEL and accelerate a new generation of cell phones, optical communication, data centers, and self-driving vehicles.

best Epitaxy Manufacturing Co. Ltd. acquired its ISO9001 certificate in April, 2020. Our new team will set our company’s goal of being in compliance with ESG standards.

Innovation and Technology

Machine improvement and fixture design

Machine improvement and fixture design | Stacked design and testing system Our company’s own technologies include our own machine improvement and fixture design, epitaxial structural design, and our own testing system, thus leading to the world’s leading ultra-high production yield and lower-than-average production cost. In addition, our company is equipped with development experience in different material systems and various device structures such that we can continue to improve production yields and reduce costs. We can also further explore the application fields of GaAs on Silicon and InP epitaxy. Our own machine improvement and fixture design: our company is equipped with years of experience in MOCVD and quite familiar with the principles of fluid and thermal fields of mechanics, so that we can gradually enhance product uniformity through machine improvement and fixture design. The excellent wavelength uniformity and controllability of our VCSEL products have significantly enhanced the competitiveness of our company’s products.

Stacked design and testing system

Our company has accumulated a large amount of epitaxial wafer structure databases of various products, and our own simulation system to help our customers with the development of different epitaxial structures for different application needs, shorten product development schedules, and reduce development costs. Our own epitaxial wafer testing system is capable of complete and quick testing of epitaxial wafers of different structures to ensure that their qualities and various indicator parameters can meet customers’ requirements. Our company’s own testing of epitaxial wafers can be done immediately after the production of every batch. With near real-time monitoring with the timely adjustment of production process parameters, we enhance production yield and quality, reduce production risks during the waiting period, and improve the machine utilization rate.

  • Wafer size: 4” and 6” GaAs substrate
  • Wavelength: 600nm~1100nm
  • Generic products: 850nm / 940nm Epi wafers for the field of proximity sensor, high power (1W/3W/5W) and 14G /25G high speed VCSEL
